A prototype is an initial creation of a product that shows the basics of what a product will look like, what the product will do, and how the product operates. 
A prototype isn't meant to be the final version; it's the rough draft form of the product.

Protocal Methods
----------------
GET - get any information
POST - to create a any message
PUT - to store any message
DELETE - remove message
PATCH - re-usable of message

Protocal code
-------------

100 -199 - Acknowledgement
200 -299 - Success
300 -399 - Warrning
400 -499 - client side error
500 -599 - server side error
